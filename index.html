The Journey

Evolution.

Originating from the town of Kursela, Katihar, my journey has been a continuous pursuit of leadership and artistic excellence. What began as a national breakthrough when I was crowned **Mr. Teen India Popular** in 2022 has evolved into a powerful multi-disciplinary career.

From walking national runways as **Model of the Year 2024** in Delhi to representing Katihar's youth inside the **Bihar Legislative Assembly** (Vidhan Sabha) as a Viksit Bharat Youth Parliament Speaker in 2026, I advocate for progressive youth leadership.

By blending public speaking distinction with high-fashion modeling, I bridge creative expression with constructive civic leadership on regional and national stages.
